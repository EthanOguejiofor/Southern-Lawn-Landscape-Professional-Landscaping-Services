About Southern Lawn & Landscape

Quality Workmanship Since 2005

Southern Lawn & Landscape Management, Inc. is a professional landscaping company serving both residential and commercial clients throughout Maryland. Based in Highland and Clarksville, we are committed to creating and maintaining beautiful, functional outdoor spaces.

Our approach is built on three core principles: meticulous planning, attention to detail, and flawless execution. We listen carefully to our clients' needs and exceed expectations through our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction.
